## 2026-02-01 - [Accessibility & Interactive Polish for Lead Dashboard]
**Learning:** Adding ARIA roles and labels to dynamic content (like progress bars in a table) and providing loading states for async actions significantly improves the perceived quality and accessibility of the interface. Global keyboard listeners (like Escape to close modals) are essential for a professional feel.
**Action:** Always check for icon-only buttons (like modal close buttons) and ensure they have aria-labels. Implement loading states for all network-bound actions to provide immediate feedback.
